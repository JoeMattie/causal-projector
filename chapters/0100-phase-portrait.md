<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7c5f-867c-d8e4fab4b0cf" -->
Lena returned on Thursday to determine whether Ruth was safe and Evan was still useful.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-767e-8257-1a517134c0cc" -->
She arrived after the home-care aide left. Ruth received her at the kitchen table with the family album closed beside her and answered every question she considered relevant. She had slept, eaten, taken her medication, and experienced no headache, weakness, nausea, or unusual sensation. She still remembered Celia. She did not consent to repeat the cognitive tasks.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-71af-954d-4002a60655a2" -->
"I was clear enough to know when he should stop examining me," Ruth said.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7a14-ae90-48e012033280" -->
Lena wrote this down. "Do you want me to discuss the run with him?"

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-77e8-bc9e-1a2957e2689d" -->
"I want you to tell him what happened. He thinks understanding and permission are the same thing."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7e01-8bb9-9c44995509ea" -->
"They aren't."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7dc7-905e-6e793de8d94e" -->
"Good," Ruth said. "You may continue."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-76cb-94f6-8da2a63f0418" -->
In the garage, Lena compared the treatment log with the independent replication. Ruth's six-minute run had produced lower absolute field, less heating, and a shallower covariance collapse than either operator trial. Its effect on autobiographical coherence had been larger than its instrument signature suggested.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7139-b113-9a217f37a3ad" -->
"Your power reduction didn't reduce the relevant coupling," Lena said.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7d79-ad6e-c873f2460a5b" -->
"Her answers were the feedback signal."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7ddd-bfad-087f7519bc57" -->
"Yes. You put identity inside the loop and then congratulated yourself for turning down the amplifier."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-773d-a6d7-d7a9cef94a56" -->
Evan accepted this because arguing would have converted an accurate statement into a dispute he could try to win.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7470-b7dd-74403df9f8ad" -->
He showed Lena the visual notes after lunch.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7649-a913-ef6dc988691d" -->
The first entry predated Ruth's run. During L-03 he had seen a faint hexagonal grain across the closed-eye darkness and had given it the weight of a floater. During Lena's replication it came back with depth in it, like tiled rooms viewed from all sides at once, and he had been sitting in a diner booth while it did, blaming the light. While the sensors lost rank in RUTH R-01, it found the edge of his vision in his mother's kitchen, over the white of a cupboard door, while he scored her answers. He had been the active subject exactly once. After that the pattern had proceeded without needing him.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7478-87d0-b47ed357a533" -->
He had omitted it because geometric afterimages were subjective, migraines existed, and writing hallucination beside cognitive enhancement would have made every other result easier to ignore.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7540-9b8b-63cc12ff6065" -->
Lena read the entries without changing expression. "Have you seen this before?"

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7c72-83f9-47139417a325" -->
Evan said yes.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7197-a97d-b34cd678d691" -->
He had not recognized the grain when it first appeared during L-03. He had recognized it completely by the time it acquired depth. Eleven years earlier, he had participated in a licensed perception study that used DMT. He had volunteered because the study paid, the monitoring looked serious, and another engineer claimed the experience was impossible to describe. Evan had interpreted this as a challenge to description.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7d30-bc65-9c16c90a50fc" -->
The session lasted minutes. It did not reveal a cosmic truth. It dismantled the ordinary priority by which one room, one body, and one sequence of moments suppressed every competing interpretation. He saw recursive architecture populated by figures that seemed to react when he attended to them. Afterward he treated the impression as a property of a brain temporarily unable to enforce its usual model.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7be7-aae0-2604a1b8d41e" -->
He had kept one page of notes.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-701d-94a4-b984be67e9e8" -->
```text
PERCEPTION STUDY, 11 YEARS AGO

not a place
not infinite, only too recursive to count at the time
figures may be visual system rendering agency from structure
attention changes what counts as foreground

strongest impression:
  being compressed into a form the scene could recognize
```

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7c38-97fe-f541ad0e36bd" -->
Lena placed the old note beside the machine-state entries. "This does not make DMT a detector."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7892-8185-c8a16a709e5a" -->
"I know."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-76c2-b0de-c12a32499330" -->
"It makes your prior experience an excellent source of expectation bias."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7e51-8dfb-f4f7d3e99ed3" -->
"I know that too."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7380-894e-12614e3f019a" -->
"Then we can test the report without giving you anything."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-74fd-9542-f6ba445c69c0" -->
They designed PHASE P-01 as a perceptual correlation experiment, not an exposure for benefit. Evan would sit in the darkened front room with an opaque mask and broadband noise. He could not see, hear, or feel the garage controls. Lena would run safe and fault builds in concealed order, then inject three classes of events after the anomalous region began.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7039-8e8d-4bd75a520887" -->
Physical phase steps advanced or retarded the heater front. Decoy steps wrote identical values into a shadow register no hardware consumed. Hold events changed neither. Evan would speak only a small vocabulary describing the geometry: LEFT, RIGHT, STILL, OPEN, CLOSED, DEEPER, SHALLOWER, FIGURE, NONE.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7af7-b4e2-d57ea2c8f210" -->
The vocabulary prevented him from narrating his way toward a match. Event timing and analysis were fixed before the first run.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7ae3-b7b7-24cb7c64b042" -->
```text
PHASE P-01: BLINDED PERCEPTUAL CORRELATION

observer: E. Hale, no display or control access
operator: L. Varga
conditions: SAFE / FAULT concealed from observer

hidden events:
  PHYSICAL LEFT / RIGHT
  DECOY LEFT / RIGHT
  HOLD

observer vocabulary:
  LEFT RIGHT STILL OPEN CLOSED DEEPER SHALLOWER FIGURE NONE

primary:
  report alignment within 2 s of hidden event
secondary:
  reported recursion depth versus covariance rank
```

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7df3-8507-8da6b716b404" -->
The safe run produced retinal grain, two vague rings, and nothing Evan could classify as a figure. He reported NONE through every hidden event. Lena did not tell him which condition had run.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-73c7-8095-adb4e3acd69e" -->
After a full thermal reset, she loaded the second concealed build.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-75b6-9138-c0645081f397" -->
For twenty minutes Evan saw the ordinary mottled darkness produced by eyes with no useful input. Then the grain organized.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7555-ac96-e8ef5a7cc103" -->
Lines joined into six-sided cells. Each cell contained a smaller arrangement that was not a copy but served the same structural role. The field acquired depth without a stable direction of view. Near and far became levels of subdivision rather than distance.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7f99-96dc-437effed1a6d" -->
"DEEPER," Evan said.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-750f-b7b5-a5681bf04735" -->
In the center, several cells began sharing edges. Their combined boundary closed around a vertical form. It had no face or fixed number of limbs. Wherever Evan tried to locate one surface, smaller versions performed the same closure beneath it.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-79e2-9a12-00a105e3178a" -->
"FIGURE."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-71e5-b875-fd3eb4c97f54" -->
The form rotated left.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7738-84ea-a5042f76aee4" -->
"LEFT."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-75cd-89c1-c7cbde786ab7" -->
It stopped. Smaller structures continued turning inside it, some left and some right, but their outer relation held.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7f83-b462-c483157543ed" -->
"STILL."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7b57-887e-03ec0a66799f" -->
The next rotation went right. Evan reported it. The figure opened through the center, revealing an additional level of nested forms. He said OPEN, then DEEPER.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7a6b-b047-3c97a20a97a4" -->
Lena's hidden sequence continued. Some events produced no response. Others changed the figure's chirality so promptly that Evan began to feel he was reading a display. He reminded himself that the display was inside his own perceptual system and therefore capable of manufacturing agreement from expectation.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7d06-b65d-ad0ad937dd6b" -->
The figure then did something outside the vocabulary.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-71a9-b199-238bbf266974" -->
Its nested surfaces separated into five braided bands. Four continued through a common turn. The fifth arrived early, crossed the others, and ended in a flat repetition. Evan experienced the arrangement not as an image to decode but as a mistake he had already understood.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7a52-8eb5-6c9bc02774f5" -->
The early band was the heater confirmation edge. The flat repetition was the shadow register accepting a phase value before the thermal front had physically responded.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7586-91fd-209205a578fc" -->
"STOP," he said.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7da3-97ba-d874b17ea07a" -->
Lena opened the safety contactor. The geometry thinned into ordinary afterimage before the RF current reached zero.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7467-ad1b-9bd38d75d267" -->
She entered the front room and removed his mask. "Why did you stop?"

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7829-8fd3-30d89fbb445d" -->
Evan drew the five bands. He explained that one confirmation event occurred before the modeled thermal response and allowed the controller's causal bookkeeping to close on a state the hardware had not yet reached.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7730-9a51-803885622c96" -->
"That isn't in your source," Lena said.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7c18-8d98-5697b44726ec" -->
"The confirmation path is. I never treated it as part of the phase loop."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7973-a266-88030489c1d2" -->
They checked the event trace. The anomalous rank collapse had begun when the fifth confirmation edge advanced into the prior thermal-response window. Evan's diagram described the timing relation within one controller tick.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7733-a147-9b0314b0e5a4" -->
The phase reports were worse for Lena's decoy events. They were not merely weaker. They remained at chance. Evan had reported twenty-one of twenty-four physical left or right steps correctly within two seconds and five of twenty-four decoy writes. Reported depth increased as measured covariance rank fell.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-76b7-aa0e-da7a91da7dad" -->
The geometry had tracked what the phase value caused, not what the register contained.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7b50-bb6d-1f697ed7e513" -->
Lena reran the analysis twice. "There could be a physical cue we haven't isolated."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7276-a361-fc017efcc8c7" -->
"Through the wall, mask, and noise?"

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7f97-8a01-4bb3ba1fe87b" -->
"Yes. An unlikely cue is cheaper than a responsive hallucination."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7ffb-b5f0-5316a83c306b" -->
"You called it responsive."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7b00-b332-c531bf3e71a0" -->
"I called your claim expensive."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-76ff-9c04-2ea99b81a9ac" -->
They tested the control insight without entering another observer state. Evan delayed the confirmation latch until the measured thermal response crossed its threshold. Ordinary trapped-field gain changed little. Loss during the phase walk fell by eleven percent. The covariance collapse deepened and arrived earlier.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-76db-af4b-3633158d6441" -->
The advice worked. It also made the anomaly stronger.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7ad0-8e29-15b6b2d50aa6" -->
Lena restored the original timing and locked the branch. "We do not accept optimization suggestions from unclassified perceptual phenomena."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7de6-a223-6fcb39fa5973" -->
"It showed me a causal edge."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-76dc-8d29-ba90e95ee844" -->
"Your brain rendered an insight after weeks of studying the system. That explanation remains available."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7101-8865-445001e2ba9b" -->
"And the hidden phase sequence?"

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-768a-84fa-c68ee19f2ad2" -->
"Remains a result. It does not grant the result a personality."

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-784f-b5e2-230c522e5bc7" -->

```text

SAFE condition:
  no structured figure reported
```

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-726d-99a8-4a406370126f" -->

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7b16-8f42-d32fa34df92a" -->

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7825-bfd7-c3d3215ae89d" -->

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-71a4-8ffe-4de5977fd5af" -->
Evan returned to the garage after Lena left. He did not power the apparatus. He replayed the recording of his own sparse reports and watched the hidden event markers appear afterward.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7d7c-aace-42381065b795" -->
LEFT. STILL. RIGHT. OPEN. DEEPER. FIGURE.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7b1b-b70f-d5d0c8e03397" -->
At the moment he had said FIGURE, no hidden event occurred. The physical phase had held steady for eleven seconds. Sensor rank continued falling, but the form's closure had not followed a command.

<!-- authorbot:block id="019f87f8-c552-7e56-aaf7-b3a010bb7bfd" -->
It had followed his attention.
