## Established continuity (Chapters 7-8)

- Lena challenged the thermal model in Evan's first low-field imaging proposal.
  Fourteen months later, he sends her the anomaly data because he trusts her to
  find the conventional explanation he missed.
- She rebuilds the metrology with her own isolated instruments, blinds the
  software conditions, and reproduces the cognitive state before operating the
  machine without Evan present.
- A private action mapping known only to Lena reverses the raw noise bias while
  preserving the favored physical action. She accepts that the effect is real
  without accepting Evan's causal-history interpretation.
- After seeing Evan's exposure frequency increase, she requires a joint hold on
  cognitive protocols, outcome targeting, and any exposure of patients or
  dependents pending replication at a neutral site.

## Established continuity (Chapter 9)

- Lena refuses to approve Ruth's proposed exposure because its identity risks
  cannot be described, then treats Ruth's immediate safety as the first priority
  after Evan breaks the hold.
- She directs Evan to disable and lock the apparatus, but the unauthorized run
  creates a durable breach between scientific replication and exploitation.

## Established continuity (Chapter 10)

- Lena converts Evan's geometric reports into a blinded phase-sweep with a
  fixed vocabulary, physical phase changes, decoy register writes, and concealed
  machine conditions.
- She accepts the perceptual correlation as a result while retaining physical
  cue and observer-inference explanations. She rejects treating the responsive
  structure as a trustworthy person or optimization source.

## Established continuity (Chapter 11)

- Lena forces every observation, assumption, and cosmological claim into a
  separate evidence category. She accepts only finite effective support in the
  observed system, not Evan's broader finite-universe claim.
- She agrees to one identification run for the unstable peripheral form with an
  automatic abort if its visible recursion depth decreases.

## Established continuity (Chapter 12)

- Lena isolates Evan's spoken depth reports from the acquisition system so a
  report below four can only open and latch a physical safety contactor. She
  retains an independent manual stop for uncertainty or protocol fault.
- She recognizes that Evan's claimed fourth level has become repetition and
  manually terminates the run after his false report prevents the automatic
  abort.
- She preserves the classifier, controller, and audio records on write-once
  storage, records that the observer defeated the safety path, and removes the
  contactor key. The breach makes her moral opposition direct and personal.

## Self-fulfilling betrayal

Once the protagonist’s internal model dominates the controller, the machine
preserves histories consistent with that model. If he suspects betrayal,
branches containing betrayal become more compatible with the attractor. His
friend eventually betrays him because nonbetrayal branches are eroded.
Delusion becomes evidence, then history.

## Validation role

In the validation ladder, a collaborator operates the machine while the
protagonist is absent. The effect follows the active controller but retains
weaker coupling through software and experiment design.

Collaborator fate: erased branch, self-fulfilling betrayal, unwilling
