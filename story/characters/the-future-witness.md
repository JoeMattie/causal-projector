## Dramatic role

A person from the child universe perceiving the genesis boundary during an
altered state. Not a version of the protagonist, though both misidentify the
encounter.

## A future observer across the genesis boundary

Near the climax, one entity is not an alternate self. It is a person in the
child universe, billions of years later, perceiving backward through the
genesis boundary during a DMT experience. Neither can render the other
accurately. The future observer sees an autonomous geometric intelligence.
The protagonist assumes another collapsing copy. Both are locally reasonable
and globally wrong.

## Place in the spine

Part V: the protagonist encounters the Future Witness and misreads the
contact as another copy. The reader can understand the cross-boundary
encounter before he does. Epilogue: in our universe, a person undergoing a
DMT experience encounters a fractal entity that still believes it is a
software engineer standing beside a failing superconducting machine.
