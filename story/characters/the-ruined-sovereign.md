## Entity nature

Each entity is a compressed family of related histories. One may contain
versions who became famous, weaponized it, cured dementia at terrible cost,
died in the garage, or transferred the pattern into institutions and
machines. The preserved invariant is not the protagonist’s face or biology
but a deeper behavioral topology: recursive modeling, curiosity, control,
and repeated construction of the projector.

## Recognition through private invariants

The reveal should accumulate through private invariants rather than arrive
as a cosmic announcement. An entity uses the exact hand movement he makes
while mentally stepping through code. Another reconstructs a childhood room
with different parents. Several share a scar, a shame response, or the
structure of a private comment in source code.
